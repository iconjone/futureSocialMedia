Out with the old.
Unlike the social networks of the past, on Cyworld - you won't meet your friends as icons or usernames on flat, 2D screens. Instead, your interactions occur in fully realized 3D spaces, with each person being represented as 3D avatars allowing individual interactivity with almost the same degree of freedom and expressiveness we're accustomed to in the real world.
